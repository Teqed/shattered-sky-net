| Level | Wealth Points |
| ----- | ------------- |
| 2     | 1 wp          |
| 3     | 3 wp          |
| 4     | 6 wp          |
| 5     | 11 wp         |
| 6     | 16 wp         |
| 7     | 24 wp         |
| 8     | 33 wp         |
| 9     | 46 wp         |

| 11    | 82 wp         |

Once per day while making your daily preparations, you can reassign your Wealth Points to purchase any of the following abilities.

::TileItemSrd
### Offensive Attunement
**Price** *2wp (+1), 8wp (+2), 18wp (+3), 32wp (+4), 50wp (+5), 72wp (+6), 98wp (+7), 128wp (+8), 162wp (+9), 200wp (+10)*\
\
You can attune to any one weapon or implement in your possession, and can change that attunement once per day. The attuned item gains a +1 to +10 effective bonus.\
\
You may exchange the effective bonus of the attuned implement for special abilities with a cost equivalent to an enhancement bonus. You must meet any feat or talent prerequisites for crafting the quality besides Craft Implement and Craft Arms.\
\
Any remaining effective bonus becomes an enhancement bonus for the attuned item (maximum +1 plus +1 per 4 levels, up to +5).\
\
Special: You may take this feature multiple times, choosing a different item each time.

::
::TileItemSrd
### Defensive Attunement
**Price** *1wp (+1), 4wp (+2), 9wp (+3), 16wp (+4), 25wp (+5), 36wp (+6), 49wp (+7), 64wp (+8), 81wp (+9), 100wp (+10)*\
\
You can attune to any one suit of armor or shield in your possession, and can change that attunement once per day. \
\
This functions otherwise as Offense Attunement.

::
::TileItemSrd
### Multi-Weapon Attunement
**Price** *2wp (+1), 8wp (+2), 18wp (+3), 32wp (+4), 50wp (+5), 72wp (+6), 98wp (+7), 128wp (+8), 162wp (+9), 200wp (+10)*\
\
**Prerequisites** Offensive Attunement (same bonus or higher)
When attuning to a weapon, you may attune to any number of additional weapons in your possession. These additional weapons only function as attuned while wielded by you. Any unattuned weapon you wield receives the maximum enhancement bonus you are eligible for.


::

### Bonus Quality
**Price** *Varies*\
\
You can add a special quality which has a gold point cost to an item you are attuned to. The wealth point cost is equal to its normal cost in gold pieces divided by 1000.

**Price** *28wp*\
